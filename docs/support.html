Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a link?

Use the Share button in Safari or any app, then select Linksly. Choose a recipient, add an optional note, and tap Queue.

What's the difference between Free and Plus?

Free users can save links and share with up to 2 recipients. Plus ($6.99 one-time) unlocks unlimited recipients, link shortening, iCloud sync, and multi-link previews.

How do I restore my Plus purchase?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is my data private?

Yes! Your contacts stay on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. Links are stored locally, and iCloud sync (Plus feature) uses your personal encrypted iCloud account. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual links by swiping left in the app. To delete all data, simply remove the app from your device. Any shortened links can be deleted from the app settings.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please email us at support@linksly.com with details about what's happening, and we'll help you out.
